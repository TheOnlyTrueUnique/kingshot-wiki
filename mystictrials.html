What is the Mystical Trial?

The Mystical Trial is one of Kingshot's most competitive PvE game modes, consisting of six distinct arenas. Each arena isolates a specific part of your account — from Heroes and Pets to Research and Governor Gear — and scores you based on how well you've developed that area.

Success across all arenas requires a well-rounded account. Neglecting any one system will show up immediately as a weak performance in its corresponding arena.

Pro Tip

Each arena tests a different part of your account. Build everything well-rounded — a strong hero but weak research will still cost you in the Knowledge Nexus. The Radiant Spire combines all sources, so it rewards players who invest evenly across every system.

🏟️
Coliseum
Arena 1 · Heroes

Tests your Heroes, Hero Gear, and Hero Exclusive Widgets. The strength of your hero roster — their levels, equipped gear, and unlocked widgets — determines your performance here. Invest in upgrading your best combat heroes for maximum impact.

Troop Ratio
50/ 10/ 40
🌿
Forest of Life
Arena 2 · Pets

Focuses on your Pets & Pet Skills. Pet skills activate automatically during combat — you just need to have trained and leveled them properly. A strong pet collection with high-tier skills is key to dominating this arena.

Troop Ratio
50/ 15/ 35
💎
Crystal Cave
Arena 3 · Governor Charms

Utilizes your Governor Charm stats. Charms are collectible boosts tied to your Governor profile that enhance specific combat and development stats. Unlocking and upgrading your charm collection is essential to performing well here.

Troop Ratio
60/ 20/ 20
📚
Knowledge Nexus
Arena 4 · Research

Tests your Academy Tech and War Academy Tech research. The deeper you've researched your tech trees — both civilian and military — the stronger you'll perform. This arena rewards long-term investment in upgrading your Academy.

Troop Ratio
50/ 20/ 30
🔥
Molten Fort
Arena 5 · Governor Gear

Challenges the effectiveness of your Governor Gear. Unlike Hero Gear, Governor Gear is worn by your governor character directly and provides base stat bonuses. The better your gear tier and enhancements, the more damage you'll deal here.

Troop Ratio
60/ 15/ 25
🌟
Radiant Spire
Arena 6 · Ultimate Challenge

The ultimate arena — combines ALL stat sources from every other arena. Heroes, Pets, Charms, Research, and Governor Gear all count here. Uniquely, you are also allowed to use your personal troops, making your troop count and quality a direct factor in your success.

Troop Ratio
50/ 15/ 35
👑 Personal Troops Allowed
